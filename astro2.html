Astrophotography Gallery

Welcome to Arsal Kashif's collection of images of the Sun, Moon and more! All of these images were taken by him using basic equipment (eg. Nikon d90, Celestron Astromaster LT70, etc).


To navigate the site, simply choose one of the tabs above and you can explore through a vast selection of images from basic wide field shots to detailed images of a total solar eclipse.


However, do note that most of the images here are not the full resolution versions. If you wish to use any of these images, please email absolutejunk00@gmail.com, and he will be able to supply you with the full resolution version of the image.
